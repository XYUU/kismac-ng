KisMAC Manual - The Traffic View

KisMAC's Traffic Grapher...

  1. This legend shows the current networks in their respective color. You may customize the informations, which are show here, in the traffic preferences.
  2. You may advice KisMAC to show either current "Signal" values or the "Packet" and "Byte" throughput. Please note that the last two are only available in passive mode.
  3. Use this popup-button to change the current scale.
  4. The most recent value is also expressed as a number.
