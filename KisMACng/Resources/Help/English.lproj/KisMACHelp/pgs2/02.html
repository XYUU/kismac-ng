Why is KisMAC an alpha version?

KisMAC is an alpha version for various reasons:
1 KisMAC is a testing platform for us.
2 Very little testing has been performed. Kismac may be unstable and/or unfinished in some areas.
3 KisMAC is not a commerical application
4 There is no security design in KisMAC - it may be insecure.
5 We develop for fun and testing is boring =)
