Why doesn't KisMAC work?

Unfortunately KisMAC is not a tool for computer newbies. It often requires a little bit of playing around to get it to work.

Possible problems are:
1The Airport card was in use BEFORE launching or WHILE running KisMAC
2Lack of administrative privileges
3Computer was not restarted after KisMAC crashed
4You did not enter your administrative password.
5You started the "Classic" eviroment.
5There are processes, which access the Airport Card directly (such as Internet Connect.app). This will keep KisMAC from reloading your Airport Driver on quit.

Solutions:
1Disable the Airport card via the Network control panel
2Gain administrative access
3Reboot the machine
4Search computer's system log for any recorded problems
5Stop "Classic"
6Quit other programs.
