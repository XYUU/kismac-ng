Why do you not support cracking of 128bit or 64 bit keys?

Actually KisMAC supports this feature, but we do not follow the marketing of most wireless companies. Their 128bit-keys are actually 104bit-secret-keys plus a 24 bit long initialization vector (IV), which is submitted in plain text. Therefore the effective key length is 104bit. The same rule applies to 64bit, which has an effective strength of 40bit.

However there are the occasional good apples. Nokia for example uses 128bit secrets plus 24bit IVs, but we do not have any hardware to test the algorithms on their machines. You could of course give us some hardware if you really want the feature...
